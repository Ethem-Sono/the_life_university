...The Life University...

..About..

  • "Life University is a youth movement that aims to support individuals facing everyday challenges, such as homework assistance and exam preparation.
  • We provide sessions where young people can engage with our professional psychologists, who have been part of our team for years. Life University offers a wealth of knowledge in the four arts, including Hip-Hop, Breakdance, rapping, writing, and scratching.
  • Our motto is 'live and let live.'
  • Founded in 2023 by Ethem Sahin, Life University provides a safe social space for young people, offering opportunities for personal development and life skills.
  • Through workshops, classes, and guidance from our mentors, we strive to impart knowledge not typically found in traditional school curricula.
  • Our goal is to foster connections and create a supportive environment where you can freely discuss any topic.
  • We are more than happy to listen and support you.

..Workshops..

  • Hip-Hop Dance Workshop: Learn the fundamentals of hip-hop, including Breakdancing, Popping, Locking, and Freestyle Dance.

  • African Dance Workshop: Experience the rich culture and rhythm of traditional African dance forms

  • Jazz Dance Workshop: Explore the expressive and dynamic moves of jazz dance

  • Fusion Dance Workshop: Combine different dance styles to create your own unique fusion routines

  • Music Production Basics Workshop: A beginner's workshop covering the fundamental concepts of music production, including DAW (Digital Audio Workstation) navigation and basic recording techniques

  • Song Arrangement Workshop: Dive into the process of arranging and structuring a song, including building verses, choruses, bridges, and more

  • Hip-Hop Dance Workshop: Learn the fundamentals of hip-hop, including Breakdancing, Popping, Locking, and Freestyle Dance

  • Vocal Production Workshop: Focus on recording, editing, and processing vocals to create polished vocal tracks in your productions

  • Street Art and Graffiti Mural Workshop: Explore the world of street art and graffiti, including spray paint techniques, tagging, and lettering styles

  • Social Skills and Communication Workshop: Improve interpersonal skills, including active listening, conflict resolution, and assertiveness training

  • Positive Psychology and Happiness Workshop: Explore the principles of positive psychology to enhance overall well-being and happiness
